1. This is a sample heatmap demo of company A's energy consumption. 

2. A week heatmap is shown at a time, you can click "TestData week1.tsv" or "TestData week2.tsv" to display different datasets. The datasets are not real energy consumption data, they are only some samples.

3. Each day is represented by a row, each row is subdivided into 24 hourly blocks. Each block is colored according to the energy consumed in that hour(For example, yellow represents the energy consumed in that hour is greater than 3units and less than 17units).
Notes: units could be any general units, such as kWh, Joules, or even monetary cost.

4. Chrome may block local tsv files, please open heatmapdemo2.html with firefox, thanks.
